Hey, welcome to the Boxxle Game Page. By now you should have already entered a username and should be ready to play. If you are still a little hazy on how the game works here is a basic summary. The goal is to get the character (blue guy) to move around and push all the crates over the dots. Once the box is over a crate, it should turn blue. If all the crates are over the dots then you will be able to advance to the next level by pressing enter.

If you do not see a character or crates, simply press enter to generate them. If you find yourself stuck on the level and have no where to go. Simply refresh the page to start the level over.

Press Space Bar to clear these paragraphs. Enjoy the Game!
